First Aid: The Life-Saving Skills Everyone Should Know

General Definition of First Aid

What is First Aid?
is the immediate care provided to someone who is injured or ill, before professional medical help arrives. Its goal is to save lives, prevent further harm, and provide basic care until specialized help is available. This can include actions like stopping bleeding, performing CPR, and treating minor injuries.



Why First Aid Matters? First aid knowledge is essential, as it can save lives and prevent injuries from worsening in emergencies. It helps people act quickly and confidently, providing care until professional help arrives. By learning first aid, individuals are empowered to make a difference, support public health, and promote safer communities.
