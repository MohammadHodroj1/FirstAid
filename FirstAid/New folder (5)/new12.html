Types of burns,their stmptoms and severity:
Degrees of burns Symptoms Risk rate
Superficial burns(first class)
  1. redness
  2. mild pain
  3. dry skin without blisters
 (1%-10%)
Moderate burns(second class)
  • severe redness
  • severe pain
  • the appearance of fluid-filled blisters
  • swelling in the affected area
 (10%-30%)
Deep burns(third class)
  1. loss of sensation due to nerve damage
  2. skin color may be waxy white or brown
  3. there is no direct pain due to the destruction of nerve endings
 (30%-70%)
Complete burns(forth class)
  1. Deep tissue damage
  2. complete loss of sensation in the affected area
  3. the skin color may be charred or black
 (70%-90%)
